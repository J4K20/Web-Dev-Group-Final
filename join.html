Join Impact100

If you are looking to join Impace100, please make an account first. This will allow you to become a volunteer and request a grant. If you wish to further support our cause, you will then have an option to donate $1000 to become a full memeber, or $500 to become a half member.



Volunteer Oppertunities

Creating an account will allow you to see oppertunities to volunteer in your area. These oppertunitites include projects, food drives, inviting other members, and helping with community events.



Membership

Pay the yearly membership price of $1000 to become a full member, allowing much more involvement in helping the community. This allows you to join our team and participate in more involved activities such as the grant acceptance process, event planning, recruiting, and other projects. Being a member also provides invitations to meetings and grant votes.
