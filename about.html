Who Are We?

At Impact 100, we strive to grow the community and support those who want to leave a lasting impact. At the end of each year, 100% of our members donations go towards grants in one of our focus areas:

  • Culture
  • Education
  • Family
  • Health & Wellness
  • Environment Protection & Recreation

How It Works

Impact100 has a large foundation of donors and members, and the collected funds from all our supporters helps up provide grants to those seeking to improve something in the community.

Since 2002, Impact 100 has given over $7 million
