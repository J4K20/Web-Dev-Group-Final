Eligibility Requirements

To be eligible for a grant, your organization must adhere to the following requirements

  • Organization or project aligns with Impact100 guidelines
  • The grant must be aligned with an Impact 100 Focus Area (Culture, Education, Environment, Family, Health & Wellness).
  • Organization or project has measurable and well defined goals
  • There must be a set plan to use the entire $100,000 grant
  • The organization must not have recieved a grant from Impact100 in the last 3 years

If you have any questions, please email us at grants@impact100.org
